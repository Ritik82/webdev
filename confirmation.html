Eiffel Tower

Description:

  1. The Eiffel Tower is a wrought-iron lattice tower located in Paris, France.
  2. It stands 324 meters (1,063 feet) tall and was completed in 1889 as the entrance arch to the 1889 World's Fair.
  3. Designed by Gustave Eiffel, it was the tallest man-made structure in the world until the completion of the Chrysler Building in New York in 1930.
  4. Today, it is one of the most recognizable landmarks in the world, attracting millions of visitors annually.
  5. The Eiffel Tower offers stunning views of the city from its observation decks and has become a symbol of France and Paris.
